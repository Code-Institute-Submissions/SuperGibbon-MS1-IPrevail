About I Prevail

I Prevail is an American metalcore band formed in Southfield, Michigan in 2013. They released their debut EP Heart vs. Mind (2014) and rose to popularity from releasing a metal cover of Taylor Swift's "Blank Space" as a single, which eventually was certified platinum in the US. The band has since released two studio albums, Lifelines (2016) and Trauma (2019) The band found success with their original material as well, with singles "Breaking Down" and "Hurricane" performing well on rock radio, and "Bow Down" being nominated for Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance. Trauma was similarly nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Rock Album the same year.

Brian Burkheiser

Singer-songwriter for the post-hardcore band I Prevail. One of the original band members and co-founder. Originally from Michigan, he grew up loving music and studied throughout his life to break in to the business. His idea to cover a popular Taylor Swift song "Blank Space" shot the band to fame over night, the video now has over 30 million views on youtube, Brian credits this video (with a pinch of luck) to the reason hes able to live his dream.

Dylan Bowman

Dylan was born and raised in Michigan and started playing guitar when he was very young. Dylan didn't know he wanted to be a musician until the chance fell in his lap, after the former guitarist of the band left, Dylan was given a shot to fill the void and fill he did. He guitar playing had brought him to the forefront of a dream he didnt know he wanted and he was living the life. His talents now offer him the chance to offer lessons to those that want to learn to play guitar through webinar weekends.

Eric Vanlerberghe

Eric is one of the founding members of the band, in his own words he bringsd the "unclean" vocals to the songs when its needed with his one of a kind scream. On more recent albums hes started to add his singing voice to tracks, even surprising fans with how great his voice is. Outside the band Eric is a huge animal lover, owning 2 cats and a dog. Eric recently started a venture in to Twitch, streaming an array of content in an effort to entertain a whole new array of fans.

Gabe Helguera

Gabe is the newest member of Iprevail, joining in 2017 to replace the former drummer he has fit in perfectly with the bands style and still being able to add his own flavour. Gabe is owner and founder of drumbeatsonline, a company trying to bring up the next generation of drummers through the academy using youtube, instagram and his podcast to reach the world.

Steve Menoian

Steve is another founding member of the band, he plays lead guitar and occasionally added some backing vocals but his real talent is in song writing, penning many of the bands greatest songs along with Brian. Outside the band life Steve is a bit of a artist, he likes to draw and paint (many of which in volve his cat).
